Undergraduate Honours Project

    Overview
    For My honours project, I have created an educational game running on android tablets. It was a touch based music puzzle game created in the Unity engine.

    Educational Aims
    The purpose of this game was to increase the amount of communication between children with Austistic Spectre Condition (ASC) and typically developing children in a classroom setting. Research showed that one of the main shared interests between the two groups are video games and music. The game therefore tries to combine these two areas to give the two groups a common topic for communication.
